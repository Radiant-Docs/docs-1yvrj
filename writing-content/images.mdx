## Two ways to add captions

Because this documentation uses MDX, you have two ways to add captions to your images:

### 1. Standard Markdown (Simplest)

Any title added in quotes after the image URL will automatically be displayed as a caption.

```md
![Alt text](/path/to/image.jpg "This is a caption")
```
