---
title: "Accordion"
---

The Accordion component allows you to create collapsible sections in your documentation. Users can expand and collapse content to focus on what they need, making it perfect for FAQs, detailed explanations, or organizing complex information.

<ComponentPreview>
  ```mdx
  <AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="What is this framework?">
      Our framework is a modern, lightweight solution for building web
      applications with excellent developer experience and performance.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="How do I get started?">
      Getting started is easy! Install the package, create a new project, and
      follow our quickstart guide in the documentation.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Is it free to use?">
      Yes, our framework is open source and free to use for both personal and
      commercial projects under the MIT license.
    </Accordion>
  </AccordionGroup>
  ```
</ComponentPreview>
